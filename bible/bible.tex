\section*{The Announcement}

\divider

\lettrine[lines=2]{C}{enturies} passed\ldots\\new powers rose\ldots

\lettrine[lines=2]{E}{dgar,} \speech{Charmed Groom}, was to take Olivia, \speech{Crimson Bride}, in matrimony! Wedding Announcements reached every Command \speech{Tower} by night-fall; Herald's Horns rang: \speech{Paths} Of Ancestry were to be cauterized in \speech{Blood} with now-forth-coming Seals Of The \speech{Guild}-pact:

\lettrine[lines=2]{E}{lenda,} \speech{Saint Of Dusk}, bore Arcane Signets of \speech{The Church} as she met with Fore-runners Of The Legion at The Vault Of The Arch-angel; Elenda breezed her way up and down Bright- and Grim-climb \speech{Path}-ways to dutiful-ly arrive at Orzhov \speech{Guild}-gate;

\lettrine[lines=2]{B}{aron,} \speech{Bertram Graywater}, opened his Imperial Seal with a shock: \speech{''\ldots\ in-person attendance at The Estate?!''} Yet, despite his anguish, he intuited a lucrative policy proposal: descending his Luxury Suites of Castle Locthwain, Bertram delved in-to Shadowy Back-streets to convene with Kamber, \speech{The Plunderer}. Bertram secured generous \speech{Life} Insurance through Kamber's Black Market Connections; only then, and with Laurine, \speech{The Diversion}, \speech{Human} thrall, to carry his luggage, he crossed Arid Mesas, tiptoe-ing Pillar- and Needle-verge \speech{Path}-ways to hesitant-ly arrive at Boros \speech{Guild}-gate;

\lettrine[lines=2]{V}{ito,} \speech{Thorn Of The Dusk Rose}, arrived last, having lugged a cumber-some Heir-loom Mirror from \speech{Blood} Crypts of \speech{Blood}-stained Mire; Vito trudged Haunted Ridges and perilous Blight- and Sear-step \speech{Path}-ways to haggard-ly arrive at Rakdos \speech{Guild}-gate.

\lettrine[lines=2]{V}{oldaren} Estate, at the end of \speech{Sun}-down Pass, was finally before them!

\section*{The Crimson Vow}

\divider

\lettrine[lines=2]{W}{edding} Festivities were in the air: Cordial \speech{Vampires} welcomed one-another over goblets of Exquisite \speech{Blood}; Welcoming \speech{Vampires} guided Charismatic \speech{Conquerors} through Elegant Parlors; \speech{Vampire} Socialites whispered centuries-old gossip; Falken-rath Gorgers and Rakish Heirs leered on as \speech{Blood} Artists painted in red; however\ldots\ from Prop Rooms of Raucous Theaters\ldots

\lettrine[lines=2]{F}{lorian} un-covered \speech{Demonic} icono-grams siphoning black mana out of The Dazzling Theater!\\\speech{Demonic Tutors} were summoning some-thing horrible! \speech{''Why didn't the Damn Detection Tower Hex-proof against this?!''} Florian cried with naïve mis-understanding.

\lettrine[lines=2]{O}{utside} The Estate, beyond Cooking Camp-sites,\\blissfully un-aware of the impending doom,\\\speech{Human} thralls toiled on their Side-quest: \speech{Catch a Fish}.

\lettrine[lines=2]{A}{nje}, \speech{Maid Of Dis-honor}, preened Olivia's dress.\\En-lightened \speech{Tutors} preached words of wisdom.\\The \speech{Sol Ring} was presented, but just then\ldots\\Crackling Doom snuffed the candle-light!

\lettrine[lines=2]{B}{lood-letters} Of \speech{Aclazotz} burst through the stained-glass of The \speech{Sacred} Foundry! Markov Barons scurried; Edgar Markov brandished The Blade Of The \speech{Blood}-chief\ldots\ but it was too Damn late, he had met his Maze's End! Victimized before the entire congregation:\\it was an Anguished Un-making!

\section*{Covenant \& Crusade}

\divider

\lettrine[lines=2]{E}{lenda}, \speech{The Dusk Rose}, was her name; what bargain she struck, what \speech{Blood} she surrendered, scriptures dare not say; only that through some \speech{Demonic}\\Dark Ritual she sacrificed her-self\ldots

\lettrine[lines=2]{E}{lenda's} Glass-Cast Heart was en-capsulated as a relic; Elenda's Hiero-phant con-tinued her work at The Temple Of \speech{Silence}, thence-named Isolated Chapel\ldots.

\vspace{0.5cm}

\begin{center}
\textbullet\quad\textbullet\quad\textbullet
\end{center}

\lettrine[lines=2]{C}{enturies} later, at The Tomb Of \speech{The Dusk Rose}, an un-likely duo would convene: Vona, \speech{Butcher Of Magan}, sought a Charismatic \speech{Conqueror} to lead her armies; Mavren Fein, \speech{Dusk Apostle}, sought a savior; both found what they sought\ldots

\lettrine[lines=2]{T}{ogether,} they held a singular obsession, Edgar's long-lost relic: The \speech{Sol Ring}! En-lightened\\\speech{Tutors} claimed it free their thirst of \speech{Human} \speech{Blood}\ldots

\lettrine[lines=2]{C}{alls} of The \speech{Blood}-line echoed through the lands, roads flushing with pilgrims: \speech{Blood}-line Keepers constructed Pyres of Heroes to test worth-iness.

\lettrine[lines=2]{S}{orin,} \speech{Imperious \speech{Blood}-lord}, despite his name, was a Cordial \speech{Vampire} and, indeed, an Indulgent Aristo-ocrat. Sorin funded the construction of Dusk Legion Dread-noughts to honor the legacy of his grand-father: 

\lettrine[lines=2]{E}{dgar} Markov's Coffin was stowed aboard as a relic; Ruthless Law-bringers along-side Sanctum Seekers and Legion Lieutenants; Lord of Lineage took helm.

\lettrine[lines=2]{I}{mperial Seals} bearing Arcane Signets of high echelons of \speech{The Church} ordained the affair, and so they tacked their sails west in pursuit of Edgar's \speech{Ring}\ldots.

\vspace{0.5cm}

\begin{center}
\textbullet\quad\textbullet\quad\textbullet
\end{center}

\lettrine[lines=2]{V}{ito}, \speech{Thorn Of The Dusk Rose}, had glimpsed prophecies from Urza's Cave; \speech{The Church} branded them heretics, but both saw un-deniability in their visions\ldots.

\section*{Blood \& Gold}

\divider

\lettrine[lines=2]{L}{egion's} Landing\ldots

\begin{flushright}
\textit{''Ixalan was no easy conquest\ldots''\\— Bishop Of Re-birth}
\end{flushright}
\lettrine[lines=2]{A}{danto}, \speech{The First Fort}, rose up-on \speech{Sun}-lit Marshes; but it was Un-claimed Territory; peace was an illusion: Knights Of The Ebon Legion vanished in-to Marsh Flats and were found En-tombed in Fetid Heaths surrounding Takenuma, \speech{Abandoned Mire}.

\lettrine[lines=2]{A}{gadeem,} \speech{The Under-crypt}, lay below one Tainted Field: \speech{Vampire} Of The Dire Moon ventured in, but Agadeem's Awakening consumed him whole\ldots

\lettrine[lines=2]{V}{ito's} occultists flourished in Shattered Sanctums beneath Three Tree City, Command \speech{Towers} proceeded with Profane \speech{Processions}, Phyrexian \speech{Towers} communed with \speech{Demonic Tutors} from Bleach-bone Verge\ldots

\begin{flushright}
\textit{''\ldots\ where bone-white cliffs meet black water\ldots''\\— Twilight Prophet, scrying}
\end{flushright}

\lettrine[lines=2]{B}{aron}, \speech{Bertram Graywater}, however, sought more profit-able ventures, founding The Queen's Bay Company. Bertram adorned him-self with Jet and Pearl Medallions as tokens of commerce: in Concealed and Secluded Court-yards, \speech{Blood}-vial Purveyors traded precious commodities; even Queen's Bay Paladins harbored Treacherous Greed\ldots

\lettrine[lines=2]{N}{ecropolis} Regents haunted Shadowy Back-streets; Carmen, \speech{Cruel Sky-marcher}, led gangs of \speech{Vampire} Cut-throats; Dracula, \speech{The Voyager}, charted new \speech{Paths} for their Dread-noughts; they would perform any Damn deed to secure their ill-gotten \speech{Treasures}\ldots

\begin{flushright}
\textit{''Church against Company. Blood against coin\ldots''\\— Bertram Graywater}
\end{flushright}

\lettrine[lines=2]{C}{rescendoing} at The Second Meat-hook Massacre, The Butcher Of Malakir painted Shizo, \speech{Death's Store-house}, red. Luminous Brood-moths ferried \speech{Blood}-ghasts to \speech{Nykthos}\ldots\ only for Night-hawk Scavengers to Re-animate them back to \speech{Earth}\ldots

\lettrine[lines=2]{Y}{et}, at Thespian's Stage, negotiations played out in farce; true intentions had been Thought-seized\ldots.

\vspace{0.5cm}

\begin{center}
\textbullet\quad\textbullet\quad\textbullet
\end{center}

\lettrine[lines=2]{W}{hile} factions warred, Clavileño and Anowon, \speech{The Ruin Sage}, entered the hence-forth-named Fell Mire to Fell the Profane \speech{Demon} residing at Urborg, \speech{Tomb Of Yawgmoth}; yet the \speech{Blood}-letting re-vealed naught\ldots\\Clavileño's answers lay in darker depths below\ldots

\lettrine[lines=2]{G}{rim} \speech{Tutors} guiding their descent in-to The Caves Of Koilos; it was there they finally discovered The Cavern Of Souls, and at its heart: The Tomb Of \speech{Aclazotz}! \speech{''Wait an hour, here-in resides no Bat God?!''} gasped Anowon, but Clavileño had suspected as such; below\ldots

\lettrine[lines=2]{S}{crub-land} ancient as Nykthos, \speech{Erebos} re-vealed Him-self wielding His Flare of Malice: He offered a third \speech{Path}: some-thing older\ldots\ hungrier\ldots

\lettrine[lines=2]{E}{\speech{rebos}} presented Clavileño with The Helm Of The Host; with this, Clavileño finally under-stood:

\begin{flushright}
\textit{''Mortality is merely a door, not a wall!''\\— Clavileño}
\end{flushright}

\lettrine[lines=2]{W}{hen} he emerged, he was changed: for-ever known as Clavileño, \speech{First of The Blesséd}!
