✨ Glow

Interactivity is communicated through luminance intensification...
...elements brighten; they do not swap hues...
...if nothing glows, nothing is interactive.
💫 Ambientidle glow
🔆 Bloomhover 30→60%
💡 Pulseattention cue

Glow, not hue-swap. Three tiers: ambient glow, hover bloom, pulse.

Axiom 4.1

On hover, box-shadow intensifies from 30% to 60% opacity. Scale: 1.0 → 1.02 cards, 1.0 → 1.05 buttons.

§4.1 Hover

Keyboard focus renders a 2px accent-colored outline at 4px offset. Visible only on :focus-visible.

🌊 Motion

Nothing linearly slides in...
...nothing sits perfectly still...
...entrances spring; idles breathe.
🏀 Spring400–600ms
🫧 Sine3–5s ∞
🔀 Crossfade300ms overlap

Springy entrances, ambient idles. Elastic beziers for elements entering; slow sine-wave pulses for persistent attention cues.

Axiom 5.1

When content changes but the container does not, the old content fades out while the new fades in, overlapping in time. No hard cuts.

Axiom 5.2

Entrance: cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1). Hover: ease 200–300ms. Crossfade: ease-in-out 300ms.
