About Ozone

Your one stop shop to understanding sustainability at UC Merced

Took second place in Engineering Service Learning's 2018 Innovate to Grow Competition

Working with UC Merced's Department of Sustainability, Ozone offers an intuitive approach towards engaging the UC Merced campus with sustainability initiatives.

With Ozone, students, faculty, and visitors can quickly and easily see a map of the UC Merced campus overlaid with statistics, facts, and reminders that help them stay informed and make smart, conscious, eco-friendly decisions.

Code

Ozone is written as a MapBox web application wrapped in ReactJS. Check out a snippet from Ozone (which is open source!) that controls icon overlays:

Ozone was crafted with a full team of 12 individuals split roughly equally between two teams: Design and Engineering. Through the teams' efforts, this product was rapidly prototyped and presented within the timeframe of one semester!
