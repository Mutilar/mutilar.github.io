🎨 Visual Pipeline

StageDescription
Orb CanvasFour radial-gradient orbs per section with coprime oscillation periods; colors from data-colors attribute
Glint CanvasSmaller, faster accent particles layered on top with independent timing
Clip RegionsEach parallax-window clips to its viewport rect; data-attention controls orb confinement
Opaque BandsSection headers with frosted glass backgrounds break the parallax flow
Glass Tilesbackdrop-filter: blur() + subtle borders + hover glow transitions
